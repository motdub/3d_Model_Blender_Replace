CONTROLS — KEYBOARD & MOUSE
Everything below is keyboard/mouse only (no GUI button).
Selection Mode
1 Vertex select mode
2 Edge select mode
3 Face select mode
Display
4 Toggle Wireframe (points/lines/X, see-through)
5 Toggle Perspective / Orthographic
View Gizmo (top-right compass)
Click X Side view (flat ortho)
Click Y Top view (flat ortho)
Click Z Front view (flat ortho)
Click center Toggle Persp / Ortho
Select
Left Click Select element
Shift + Left Click Add/remove from selection
A Select all
B Box select (drag, click to finish)
Transform (flat by default)
G Grab / Move (slides flat on ground)
R Rotate
S Scale
X / Y / Z Lock to axis (Y or Z = move vertically)
Left Click Confirm   Right Click / Esc Cancel
Modeling
E Extrude (stays flat, then auto-grab)
W Subdivide selected edges/faces
K Knife: click edges to cut, Enter/Esc finish
F Fill (edge / face)
M Merge selected verts
X Delete selected
Ctrl + Z Undo
Right Click Context menu (Subdivide/Knife/etc)
Camera & 3D Cursor
Middle Drag Orbit around 3D cursor
Scroll Zoom in / out
Shift + Right Click Place 3D cursor (orbit pivot)
Shift + Left Click (empty space) Place 3D cursor
Shift + Middle Drag Slide 3D cursor along surfaces
Arrow Keys Fly forward/back/strafe
PageUp / PageDown Fly up / down
Export
Ctrl + E Export .glb (always solid faces)
